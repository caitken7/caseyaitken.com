About Me

I believe technology and eCommerce has the power to transform lives, which is why I love helping people and brands succeed online.

I've had the privilege of working with many talented individuals in professional digital agencies, providing solutions for clients of various sizes across multiple industries, in addition to building my own sites and eCommerce stores.

My most recent role: Lead Developer for US-based Shopify agency Lunar Solar Group, managing and contributing to a team of developers in providing web solutions for Shopify stores.

I've also built and operated multiple Shopify stores, doing everything including theme development, to digital marketing, to copyrighting.

There's always something new to learn. My current areas of learning include Shopify app development and headless eCommerce.
